Sign Language Recognition with Machine Learning

Introduction

Welcome to our article highlighting a significant breakthrough in machine learning for sign language recognition. Our research, initially presented at the prestigious International Conference on Communication, Devices and Networking (ICCDN) 2022, has now been successfully published in the esteemed journal Advances in Communication, Devices and Networking, published by Springer Nature.

Title: "Sign Language Recognition with Machine Learning: A Breakthrough Approach"

Conference: International Conference on Communication, Devices and Networking (ICCDN) 2022

Published in: Springer Nature's Advances in Communication, Devices and Networking

Publication pages: 297–308

Article link : https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-981-99-1983-3_29#citeas
